About Section

"Natalie Project" offers a mix of exploration, puzzle-solving, and survival mechanics. Players must navigate through treacherous environments, solve intricate puzzles, and uncover hidden clues to progress through the game. The island is filled with supernatural entities, each with its own unique abilities and weaknesses, making every encounter a nerve-wracking experience. Players must use their wits and limited resources to survive and unravel the island's mysteries.
